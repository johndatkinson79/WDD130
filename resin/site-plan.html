Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to bring beautiful hand crafted gifts and more to a wider customer base. To introduce customers to a more environmentally friendly alternative to plastic for a wide range of products. To increase sales by 20%.

Audience

The audience will be the older generations that recall times when craftsmanship produced high quality gifts and products. Those who value spending more money for a high quality product that lasts.

Home page

Welcome to T&A Emporium website where a wide range of resin products are available to you. Our methods of using high grade epoxy resin means we can provide you with a gift at affordable prices without losing any of the high quality you would expect. With T&A Emporium you’re not just buying a gift, you’re buying a gift that lasts a lifetime. Our range of gifts include coasters which are uniquely designed, easy to keep clean and wont scratch or mark your furniture to, fashionable and stylish jewellery that won’t break, chip, fade, need constantly cleaning or irritate your skin. Or how about never losing your place in your book ever again, with one of our funky and solid bookmarks? All our products are securely packaged using environmentally friendly packaging which you can put into your recycling bin. With next day delivery available on a wide range of items what are you waiting for? Don’t be shy, come shop and give us a try!

About Us

Hi there! I’m Nicky and I’m the founder of T&A Emporium. Producing gifts from resin has been a passion and hobby of mine for many many years and I’m excited to use that passion for you. My business started shortly after I got married and one of my husbands’ friends was really amazed by the unique wedding favour, we gave our guests. He asked me to make something similar for him for his wedding and I agreed. From that wedding came another other order, then an order for a gift for a grandchild, then BOOM. Suddenly my inbox was being flooded with requests for gifts, wedding favours and memorabilia. I started to run a small market stall in my local town in Lancashire, and then the COVID pandemic swept the UK, and the full lockdown came into force. Lockdown meant I could no longer trade so my market stall stopped however, the orders and requests for resin products did not! COVID gave me the opportunity to put my business online and reach a wider audience. I am lucky to be able to say the business has grown from strength to strength since.
